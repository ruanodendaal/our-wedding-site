Wedding Ceremony

March 21st, 2015

We have chosen Christ Church Chorleywood for our wedding ceremony, it is a beautiful church and hope you all enjoy it too.

The ceremony will begin at 1:00pm, I'd advise getting in early to get the best seats. From what I hear you're going to want to see Jen's dress!

The address is:
Christ Church, The Common
Chorleywood
Hertfordshire
WD3 5SG


Directions: (map below)
  • From the M25 J18 take Rickmansworth Rd towards Amersham
  • You will come to a cross roads with traffic lights next to a Shell petrol station
  • Drive straight over and Christ Church entrance is the next turning on your left past The Gate pub
